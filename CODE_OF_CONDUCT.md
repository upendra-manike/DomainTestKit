# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and inclusive
- Accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, abusive language, or personal attacks
- Public or private intimidation
- Publishing others' private information without consent

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within project spaces and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project.

## Reporting

Please report unacceptable behavior by opening a private security advisory or contacting maintainers through repository channels.
